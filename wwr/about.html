Our Mission at Cedar Rafting

Cedar Rafting is dedicated to delivering exceptional outdoor experiences that balance adventure, education, and environmental respect. We strive to make every trip—from calm floats to extreme rapids—an opportunity for guests to build confidence, teamwork, and lasting memories on the water.

History

Founded in 1987, Cedar Rafting began as a small family-run rafting company with a passion for sharing the beauty of local rivers. What started with a single guide and a handful of rafts quickly grew into a trusted adventure destination, known for combining thrilling whitewater experiences with scenic, educational river tours.

Over the decades, Cedar Rafting has expanded its fleet, developed multiple river paths for all skill levels, and built a reputation for safety, expert guidance, and unforgettable outdoor adventures. Today, we continue to honor our roots by offering experiences that connect guests to nature, challenge their limits, and create lasting memories on the water.
