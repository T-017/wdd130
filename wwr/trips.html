Cedar Rafting River Trips

At Cedar Rafting, we offer five unique river paths designed for every type of adventurer—from those seeking a calm, scenic journey to thrill-seekers ready to take on roaring rapids. Each route showcases the beauty of our waterways while delivering a safe, memorable experience led by our certified guides. Whether you're here to take in the beauty of the forested riverbanks or to conquer the wildest rapids, Cedar Rafting has the perfect path waiting for you. Adventure, discovery, and unforgettable memories begin on the water.

The Woodland Walker route is Cedar Rafting's most tranquil and family-friendly river experience. This gentle float drifts through calm waters shaded by towering cedar trees and surrounded by lush forest scenery. Perfect for beginners, families with children, or anyone seeking a quiet escape, the route emphasizes relaxation and discovery over speed and splashes.

The Rapid Descent route is where excitement and skill meet. Designed for experienced paddlers or those ready to take their rafting to the next level, this route delivers an unforgettable mix of class III-IV rapids, swift currents, and technical maneuvers through rugged canyon terrain. The journey winds through steep rock walls and narrow channels, with fast drops and surging waves that keep your adrenaline high from start to finish.

The White Waiver Rapids is Cedar Rafting's most extreme and exhilarating route—designed for seasoned rafters ready to take on the ultimate challenge. This full-day adventure features class IV-V rapids, steep drops, and fast-moving channels that demand strength, coordination, and teamwork. The river twists through rugged canyon walls and narrow passages, with powerful waves and unpredictable turns that will keep even veteran paddlers on edge.

Woodland Walker Our most relaxed route, Woodland Walker offers a peaceful float through quiet stretches of river surrounded by towering cedar forests. Ideal for families, beginners, or anyone wanting to enjoy nature at an easy pace, this trip includes informative commentary from your guide about the local wildlife, plant life, and the history of the region. 0-1
Cedar Sights Designed for those who want a balance between relaxation and light adventure, Cedar Sights features gentle currents and mild ripples perfect for casual paddling. Along the way, you'll pass scenic overlooks, nesting bird habitats, and natural rock formations that make this route a photographer's favorite. 2-3
Cedar Rapids A step up in excitement, Cedar Rapids combines stretches of calm water with lively class II-III rapids. It's a great choice for groups or adventurous families looking for a mix of splashes, teamwork, and fun. Expect a few surprises around the bends—nothing too wild, but enough to get your heart pumping. 4-6
Rapid Descent This fast-moving route is built for experienced paddlers and thrill-seekers. Featuring class III-IV rapids, Rapid Descent takes you through narrow channels and powerful waves that demand focus and coordination. It's an exhilarating ride through stunning canyon scenery that rewards both skill and courage. 7-8
White Waiver Rapids The ultimate challenge at Cedar Rafting, this expert-only route delivers class IV-V whitewater action with steep drops, roaring waves, and non-stop adrenaline. Guided by our most seasoned rafting instructors, this intense full-day journey promises the ride of a lifetime for those ready to test their limits. 9-10
