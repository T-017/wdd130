Skill levels based on river intensity (ranging from 0 being calm to 10 being roaring)
River Paths Details Skill level
Woodland Walker The woodland walker is a calm and relaxing tour of the woodland area. Often a favorite of families with small children or elderly memebers. 0 - 1
Cedar Sights Cedar Sights boasts some of the most beautiful woodland viewings while also providing an entry level experince into rapids rafting. Often used as the path for large groups or teaching beginner rafters. 2 - 3
Cedar Rapids Cedar rapids is our most iconic river path which provides an exhilerating white water rapids challenge for those with exeperince rafting. 4 - 6
Rapid Descent Rapids Descent is one of our more challenging river courses that includes multiple rapid falls. 7 - 8
White Waiver Rapids White Waiver Rapids does require a waiver to be signed. This path goes through the most violent and treacherous parts of the river. It is only recommened for skilled individuals with years of experience in white water rafting. 9 - 10
